Eleni Freskou

Is a dance teacher and dancer who has been dancing for over a decade. She has studied and gained inspiration from numerous great dance pedagogues, improvisators and choreographers, such as Vitoria Kotsalou, David Zambrano and Olga Spiraki. She has created various video - dance and dance - performance pieces and taught to many little and not so little dancers in Athens.

She has received a dance teacher diploma from “Aktina Professional dance school” after a 3-year long intensive program (2023) and a Bachelor’s degree in English Language and Literature from the “National Kapodistrian University of Athens” (2024). Since then she keeps on deepening and expanding her practice as well as her creativity in her own artistic works by doing daily research and experimentation and attending workshops all over Europe.

In 2025 she taught a “Dance, reflect and play” workshop to about 20 dancers in the "Spring yoga & dance festival of Ostrava" and she received very positive and sweet feedback from the participants.
